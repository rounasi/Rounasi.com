One Drop Analytics Business Intelligence Application

• • • •

OVERVIEW

This project was about designing a Business Intelligence software for One Drop Analytics.
One Drop Analytics is Business Intelligence software with a comprehensive database of 352000 companies and their data across ASEAN, India and Australia markets. It provides real-time data on activities, profiles and news of companies and industries.

  • Users can share and discuss everything in real-time with their colleagues/team-mates
  • Access to historical data, activities, news and more
  • 1000+ sources capturing company profiles, jobs, tenders, news, partnerships, contacts
  • Company activity on multiple dimensions such as growing operations, entering markets

Read the Case Study

THE PROBLEM

The challenge was that their old version of the software was lacking usability and interactive design. It was difficult to use and the support team spent a significant amount of time answering questions about how to use certain features.

DISCOVERY PHASE

Working closely with the product, sales and user research team, the discovery phase was aimed at gaining a deep understanding of the current system, to uncover user needs and business requirements and to agree on a common vision and scope for the project.We focused to understand the software's target users and their behaviours. I used Interview and survey technique to understand user's pain points in using the old version and their ideal expectation from a business intelligence software. I also used Persona technique to observe the user behaviour while using the software. The old version was web based but we found that majority of the users (who were sales persons) preferred to have the sales intelligence data on their iPad for easy access on the go.

Next step was planning stage. I planned out the workflow, timeline and also designed the structure of the UI with the potential sitemap. I used a card sorting approach to analyse what would be the best data structure for users.

DESIGN PHASE

During the design phase I developed the new structure and visual design framework for the application. I worked closely with the research team to verify early design concepts as well as more refined designs in several rounds of user testing. Based on users' feedback I refined the design and finalized the prototype.

IMPLEMENTATION PHASE

During the build phase, I made sure designs were implemented correctly and resolved design problems that came out during development. I then worked across the whole site ensuring that our HTML and CSS was clean, concise and well structured, utilising Less to make our CSS as modular and re-useable as possible and model it on the 'OO' methodology of CSS.

THE RESULT

The users’ reactions to the private beta system were very positive and decreased customer support requests by %240.
